Craft Souls

What if Minecraft and Dark Souls had a child.  This is what that is.  No way associated with Mojang or Microsoft.  All properties belong to their respective owners.  This is just a fan game made from my love of Minecraft and Dark Souls.

Really recommend you play with a controller. (xbox controller supported)

Their might be a few bugs here and there.  Report them in the comments.

Basic Controls (Controls will be displayed in game still.)

I - Inventory

View Button (controller) - Inventory

Arrow Keys - Navigate Inventory

D-pad (controller) - navigate inventory

WASD - Move

TAB - Lock on

LMG - Attack
